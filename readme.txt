== Description ==

WP promoter is wordress plugin which allows you to promote your web services to your users.
You can place Top bar , Left side bar , Right side bar , bottom bar with the Useful message and the links which floats on your screen.
It is very eye catching display of the services to your user.
Plugin also has various features such as customizing the colors etc.
After few seconds , Plugin also has functionality to display popup to your Users.
You can also notify your users about important notices on your blog. 
All this functions helps you to promote your blog.

A few notes about the sections above:

*   Provide easy promotion for your services
*   Enable to your blog to notify your user
*   Eye catching display of promotional bars and notification bars 
*   Also has functionality to display promotional popups
*	View Statistics of response of your visitors.
*	Setup your popup width and height
